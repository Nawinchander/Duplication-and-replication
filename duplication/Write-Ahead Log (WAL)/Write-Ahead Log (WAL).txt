Real-World Inspiration

Used in systems like Amazon DynamoDB, Google Spanner, and databases like PostgreSQL.

 Core Idea

Instead of directly copying data:
 Every write is first duplicated into a log (WAL)
 That log is then used to rebuild or sync data anywhere


Advanced JS Simulation

This models:

Durable write logging
Replay-based duplication
Fault recovery


Why This Is FAANG-Level
✅ 1. Durable Duplication (Not Just Copying)

✅ 3. Recovery Mechanism
